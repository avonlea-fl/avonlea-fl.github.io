# Smith Lake

## Welcome Home

Welcome to Avonlea, a community rich in natural beauty and history. Here, nature remains unspoiled, offering a serene environment for residents. From the ancient trees to the shores of Smith Lake, Avonlea is a sanctuary of preservation.

Situated at one of the highest elevations in Marion county, the community is perfectly positioned adjacent to the beautiful Smith Lake. This prime location offers residents private access to the water, ensuring solitude and peace, and maintaining a sanctuary for all who call Avonlea home.

### Community Features

* **Lot Sizes:** The community features spacious home sites ranging from two to over eight acres, with an average of five acres, ensuring privacy and quality of life.
* **Lake Access:** Homeowners enjoy guaranteed access to the private lake via two common areas, featuring a dock, ramp, and sandy beach.
* **Horses & Pets:** Avonlea is an equestrian-friendly community. Horses are welcome as specified in the deed restrictions, and the terrain is perfect for our four-legged friends.

**Enjoy life at Avonlea....a finer way of living.**
